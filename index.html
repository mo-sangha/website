Free since 2013

MeditationOnline.org has been an open meditation group since 2013, totally free of charge. We started off on Reddit's /r/Buddhism subreddit as "redditations." Our online sessions are safe spaces where you can join us from anywhere in the world. There is no expectation of donations or payment of any kind.
